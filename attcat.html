ATTCAT

Summary

ATTCAT is a collaborative forum where scholars and students meet annually to document and translate technical terminology used in Traditional Chinese Architecture. Sponsored by Southeast University School of Architecture in Nanjing and NYU Shanghai, ATTCAT workshops bring together an international team, led by Chen Wei, Zhuge Jing, Zuo Lala, and Tracy Miller, to research terms, collate translations, and advance architectural understanding. Workshops also engage participants from the Chinese Monuments Workshop, a collaboration among NYU Shanghai, Southeast University, and Vanderbilt University.
