ATTCAT

Summary

The ATTCAT project was established to develop the first digital thesaurus of Chinese architecture terminology in the English-speaking world. Beginning in 2017, our mission is to unlock the meanings of technical terms used by builders, craftsmen, and officials through both textual and visual examples. Initiated by Chen Wei 陳薇, Zhuge Jing 諸葛净and Tracy Miller 梅晨曦, our annotations have been available through the AS web portal since 2018, with abbreviated entries available through the Getty Art and Architecture Thesaurus. Longer AS entries include Illustrations, historical examples, and a bibliography of modern and premodern sources included in order to facilitate and inspire future research. Annual ATTCAT meetings are led by Professors Chen Wei, Zhuge Jing, Zuo Lala 左拉拉, and Tracy Miller and are generously sponsored by Southeast University School of Architecture in Nanjing 南京東南大學建築學院 and, since 2021, NYU Shanghai.
