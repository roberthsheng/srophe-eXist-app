Purpose & Mission

The Architectura Sinica Executive Committee is dedicated to advancing the study and accessibility of Traditional Chinese Architecture through innovative digital resources and collaborative research. The committee’s mission is to guide the development of a comprehensive, public research portal that supports scholarly exploration and engagement with China’s architectural heritage.
